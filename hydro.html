ABOUT THIS LOGO

This logo is strongly connected to the Quiet Revolution, which was a period of intense government secularization, socio-political and culural change, and the start of an "etat providence" or a state-run welfare state. The Union Nationale party (a conservative quebecois political party that aligned with Quebecois autonomism) was one of the centers for the anachrononistic ideologies that were running Quebec in that time.

1960

June 22, 1960, the liberal party under Jean Lesage won the election and the Union Nationale party no longer had complete control. The Quebec Liberal Party had plans for major reform to try to transform and "redefine the role of francophone society in Canada."
